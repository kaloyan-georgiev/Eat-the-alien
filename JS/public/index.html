In the game you control the pink alien called Tentacles.

You can move tentacles using the WASD keys.
When you press SHIFT while moving you become 3 times faster at the expense of 60 Stamina points per second.

Your goal is to smash as much flying green aliens as possible while also avoiding the enemy aliens(the big eyes)
